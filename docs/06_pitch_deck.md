## Slide 1 — Title

**FlexPay**
*The financial operating system for migrant workers in the Gulf.*

Mobile-first. Voice-first. UAE-licensed.

## Slide 2 — The problem

Ten million migrant workers in the Gulf share three problems:

1. **Expensive remittance.** Average corridor fee: 5–7%. On AED 2,000/month, that's a month's groceries lost per year.
2. **No credit history.** A worker earning AED 3,000 for a decade still cannot get a AED 500 loan. Informal lenders fill the gap at 30%+ rates.
3. **Cash economy traps.** Paid in cash, saving in cash, sending cash home — no digital footprint to build from.

The result: financial lives stuck in place for the people who power the region's economy.

## Slide 3 — Why now

- **Regulatory tailwind.** CBUAE Stored Value Facility regime active; sandbox accepting applicants.
- **Identity rails.** Emirates ID + UAE PASS enable instant digital KYC.
- **Distribution.** Smartphone penetration among migrants now > 95%.
- **AI cost collapse.** Voice interfaces viable at unit economics that work for AED 3,000 incomes.

The wedge opens once every decade. It's open now.

## Slide 4 — The product

One app, six integrated products:

1. **Wallet** — multi-currency, instant P2P
2. **Card** — virtual on day 1, physical on KYC Tier 2
3. **Remittance** — 5 corridors at < 1% margin
4. **EWA** — earned wages on-demand
5. **Credit** — alt-data micro-loans, AED 500–10K
6. **Hafiza** — digital rotating savings circles

All usable by voice. All available in Arabic, English, Hindi, Urdu, Tagalog, Bengali.
